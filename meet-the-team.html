Dr. Ranjana Raghunath

Chief Dental Surgeon

With an illustrious career spanning since 1977, Dr. Ranjana Raghunath stands as a pillar of dental excellence and trust in Dharmapuri. For over four decades, she has been dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate dental care, blending time-honored expertise with a commitment to modern techniques.

Dr. Ranjana is renowned for her gentle approach and unwavering dedication to her patients' oral health and well-being, fostering generations of healthy, confident smiles within the community she proudly serves. Her legacy is built on experience, precision, and a genuine care for every patient.

Dr. Vinayak Raghunath

Dental Implantologist & Periodontist

Dr. Vinayak Raghunath is a highly accomplished dental implantologist and qualified periodontist with over a decade of experience in implantology. He completed his specialized dental implant course at RGUHS University in 2010 and has since been recognized for his expertise.

As the youngest dental faculty at The Oxford Dental College, he received prestigious university grants to conduct implant research and was awarded the "Junior Pro-active Academician" award in 2016. With a specialization in Single Flapless and Full Mouth Implants, Dr. Raghunath employs advanced digital planning to ensure the highest level of patient care and satisfaction.
