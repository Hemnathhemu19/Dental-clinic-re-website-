Aligners & Braces

Dreaming of a straighter smile? Discover our clear aligners and modern braces.

A perfectly aligned smile is more than just an aesthetic boost—it plays a vital role in your overall oral health. Misaligned teeth can lead to difficulties in cleaning, an increased risk of tooth decay, and jaw strain. At Dr. Ranjana's Dental Centre, we offer advanced orthodontic solutions tailored for teens and adults alike.

Clear Aligners

For a discreet and comfortable treatment, we offer premium clear aligners. These custom-made, virtually invisible trays gently shift your teeth into the correct position over time. They are removable, allowing you to eat your favorite foods and maintain your regular oral hygiene routine without the hassle of brackets and wires.

Modern Braces

We also provide traditional and ceramic braces, which are highly effective for correcting complex bite issues. Today's braces are smaller, more comfortable, and less conspicuous than ever before.
