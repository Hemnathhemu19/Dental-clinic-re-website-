Dental Implants

Regain a complete smile with natural-feeling, permanent teeth replacements.

Missing teeth can impact your confidence, speech, and ability to enjoy your favorite foods. Dental implants represent the pinnacle of modern restorative dentistry, providing a permanent and highly aesthetic solution to tooth loss.

What are Dental Implants?

An implant is a small titanium post that is surgically positioned into the jawbone beneath your gums. Once in place, it fuses with your natural bone, creating a sturdy foundation for a replacement tooth (crown), a bridge, or even a full denture. Because they integrate with your bone, implants look, feel, and function just like your natural teeth.

Our Expertise

At Dr. Ranjana's Dental Centre, we utilize precision implantology to ensure optimal placement and aesthetic results. Whether you need a single tooth replacement or full-mouth restoration, our experienced team provides a seamless and comfortable experience from consultation to final restoration.
