Root Canal Treatment

Expert care to relieve discomfort and restore health with precision.

Severe tooth pain or sensitivity shouldn't be ignored. It's often a sign that the inner pulp of your tooth has become infected or inflamed due to deep decay, repeated dental procedures, or a crack or chip in the tooth. Root canal treatment is a highly successful procedure designed to eliminate this infection, save your natural tooth, and alleviate your pain.

A Pain-Free Experience

Despite common misconceptions, modern root canal therapy is very similar to having a routine filling and can usually be completed in one or two appointments. Utilizing advanced anesthetics and precision tools, we ensure the procedure is as comfortable and efficient as possible.

The Process

During the treatment, the infected pulp is carefully removed. The inside of the tooth is then cleaned, disinfected, and filled with a biocompatible material to seal the space. Finally, the tooth is restored—typically with a crown—to return it to its full strength and natural appearance.
